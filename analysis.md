# Campaign Performance Analysis

## CTR Trend
The line chart shows a steady increase in CTR from **3.35% → 3.48% → 3.56%**.  
➡️ This indicates that ad relevance and targeting improved week by week.

## Conversions
Conversions grew from **60 → 75 → 82** across three weeks.  
➡️ The campaign is successfully driving more customer actions, showing strong engagement.

## Average CPC
Average CPC decreased from **₹19.20 → ₹18.70 → ₹18.10**.  
➡️ This reflects better cost efficiency — the campaign is achieving more clicks at a lower cost.

## Key Insights
- 📈 CTR improvement suggests better keyword targeting and ad copy optimization.  
- 🎯 Conversion growth shows the landing page and funnel are effective.  
- 💰 Declining CPC highlights improved ROI and budget utilization.  

## Recommendations
- Continue optimizing ad copy and keywords to sustain CTR growth.  
- Scale budget allocation to maximize conversions while CPC remains low.  
- Experiment with A/B testing on landing pages to push conversion rate higher.
